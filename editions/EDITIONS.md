# ZILFIT Five Editions

## 1. CALM
Purpose:
Support calm, relaxation, sensory comfort, and gentle grounding.

Design direction:
- Soft midfoot stimulation
- Gentle plantar massage geometry
- Reduced harsh pressure points
- Smooth internal contact texture

## 2. VITAL
Purpose:
Post-exertion recovery, athletic fatigue support, energy restoration, and foot decompression after training or matches.

Design direction:
- Heel recovery basin
- Arch recovery bridge
- Forefoot release grid
- Thermal ventilation lattice
- Recovery-focused plantar stimulation

## 3. FOCUS
Purpose:
Support focus, alertness, toe freedom, and sensory clarity.

Design direction:
- Hallux sensory zone
- Toe freedom geometry
- Light forefoot stimulation
- Stable but responsive contact feel

## 4. BALANCE
Purpose:
Support stability, pressure distribution, gait balance, and confidence while standing or walking.

Design direction:
- Medial/lateral stability zones
- Pressure redistribution geometry
- Wider support footprint when needed
- Balance-oriented outsole contact map

## 5. FEMME
Purpose:
Female-specific biomechanical and sensory footwear system based on pelvis, gait, Q-angle, foot pressure, emotional comfort, and recovery needs.

Design direction:
- Narrower heel containment when needed
- Wider forefoot comfort where needed
- Softer sensory stimulation
- Female gait and pelvic biomechanics mapping
- FEMME can combine with CALM, VITAL, FOCUS, or BALANCE
