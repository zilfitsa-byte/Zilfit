# Z-UX Live Brief v1

Agent:
Z-UX

Mission:
Design the first conversion-focused mobile customer flow for ZILFIT.

Business Goal:
Support a fast, guided, low-friction purchase journey for the first 100 successful footwear orders.

Core Product Focus:
ZILFIT VITAL-RECOVER

Primary User Type:
A customer who wants a fast, confidence-building smart footwear experience with minimal confusion.

Required Flow Scope:
1. Welcome / first impression
2. Goal selection
3. Guided scan intro
4. Foot scan capture
5. Scan result summary
6. Recommended product
7. Fast checkout
8. Post-purchase follow-up

UX Priorities:
- mobile-first clarity
- minimal text
- low friction
- fast completion
- high trust
- scan guidance
- conversion focus
- no medical overclaiming

Constraints:
- do not design for multiple products yet
- do not add complex onboarding
- do not require account creation before checkout
- do not use medical-treatment wording
- keep the first purchase path simple

Output Requirements:
- primary_user_goal
- flow_type
- screen_sequence
- friction_points
- ux_recommendation
- conversion_risk
- next_design_step

Success Standard:
The flow should feel simple enough that a first-time user can understand the product, complete scan intake, and reach checkout without confusion.
