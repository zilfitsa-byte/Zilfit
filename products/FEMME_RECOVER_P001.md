# ZILFIT FEMME-RECOVER P001

Product type:
Full 3D-printed female-specific recovery and emotional comfort footwear.

Target users:
- Women after training
- Women after long standing
- Women needing comfort, stability, and sensory recovery
- Female athletes
- Women with different gait and foot pressure patterns due to pelvis and Q-angle differences

Not intended for:
- Medical diagnosis
- Hormonal treatment claims
- Disease treatment
- Injury prevention claims before clinical validation

Core idea:
A female-specific full 3D-printed shoe that maps female gait, pelvis-related biomechanics, plantar pressure, and emotional comfort signals into customized internal sensory and recovery geometry.

Core zones:
1. Female Heel Containment Zone
2. Soft Arch Support Zone
3. Forefoot Freedom Zone
4. Medial Stability Support
5. Gentle Sensory Massage Map
6. FEMME Emotional Comfort Layer

Primary outputs:
- Female biomechanics map
- Emotional comfort translation
- Plantar sensory map
- CAD recommendation
- Test protocol
- Patent notes
