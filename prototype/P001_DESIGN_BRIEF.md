# ZILFIT P001 Design Brief v0.1

Purpose:
Define the first non-printing prototype direction for ZILFIT.

Printing status:
No printing yet.

CAD status:
No final CAD yet.

Current objective:
Prepare two patent-ready, research-backed product paths before any physical sample:

1. ZILFIT VITAL-RECOVER P001
2. ZILFIT FEMME-RECOVER P001

---

# Product Path 1: VITAL-RECOVER P001

## Target User
Athletes and active users after:
- football matches
- training sessions
- gym sessions
- long runs
- long standing
- high foot fatigue

## Core State
Post-exertion fatigue.

Typical user words:
- heavy feet
- tired
- drained
- hot feet
- pressure
- wants recovery
- wants decompression
- wants comfort after effort

## Main Design Goal
Create a full 3D-printed recovery footwear concept that supports post-exertion comfort through plantar stimulation geometry, pressure redistribution, ventilation, and recovery-oriented support.

## Required Zones

### 1. Heel Recovery Basin
Purpose:
Support grounding and heel decompression after effort.

Design direction:
- slightly deeper heel cradle
- pressure-distributing geometry
- stable rearfoot containment
- no sharp stimulation nodes

### 2. Arch Recovery Bridge
Purpose:
Support midfoot comfort without hard medical orthotic claims.

Design direction:
- adaptive arch contact
- gradual support transition
- lattice density based on load
- no fixed number without Z-Physics

### 3. Midfoot Calm Channel
Purpose:
Create gentle sensory stimulation across the midfoot.

Design direction:
- smooth wave-like internal geometry
- low irritation risk
- sensory comfort map
- reflexology-inspired only, not medical

### 4. Forefoot Release Grid
Purpose:
Allow toe-off relief and forefoot decompression.

Design direction:
- flexible forefoot
- toe freedom
- soft pressure breakup
- avoid high nodules under metatarsal heads

### 5. Thermal Vent Lattice
Purpose:
Support heat and moisture comfort after effort.

Design direction:
- open lattice
- breathable geometry
- printability checked by Z-Printability

### 6. Sensor-Ready Capsule Cavity
Purpose:
Prepare future smart capsule integration without installing electronics in P001.

Design direction:
- no electronics in P001
- cavity placeholder only
- no wiring dependency
- no production integration yet

## Required Agent Route
Hermes -> Z-Research -> Z-Bio -> Z-Physics -> Z-NeuroFoot -> Z-CAD -> Z-Printability -> Z-Sim -> Z-Claims -> Z-Patent

# Product Path 2: FEMME-RECOVER P001

## Target User
Women needing post-effort, long-standing, or emotional comfort footwear.

Target situations:
- after training
- after long standing
- after walking
- work fatigue
- daily sensory comfort
- female athlete recovery

## Core State
Female-specific recovery and sensory comfort.

Typical user words:
- foot pressure
- tired feet
- needs softness
- needs stability
- forefoot pressure
- heel looseness
- needs emotional comfort
- wants gentle stimulation

## Main Design Goal
Create a full 3D-printed female-specific recovery footwear concept that accounts for female gait, pelvis/Q-angle context, foot shape, pressure distribution, emotional comfort, and gentle plantar sensory geometry.

## Required Zones

### 1. Female Heel Containment Zone
Purpose:
Improve heel confidence and containment.

Design direction:
- narrower heel option when needed
- soft containment
- avoid pressure on Achilles area
- stable but not restrictive

### 2. Soft Arch Support Zone
Purpose:
Support comfort and balance without medical claims.

Design direction:
- gradual support
- lower irritation risk
- adaptive to arch type
- Z-Physics must justify geometry

### 3. Forefoot Freedom Zone
Purpose:
Account for forefoot comfort and pressure sensitivity.

Design direction:
- adequate toe box
- pressure relief under metatarsal area
- flexible forefoot geometry
- no hard nodes under sensitive zones

### 4. Medial Stability Support
Purpose:
Support stability experience related to gait and alignment.

Design direction:
- medial/lateral balance
- Q-angle aware design logic
- pressure redistribution
- not an injury prevention claim

### 5. Gentle Sensory Massage Map
Purpose:
Translate emotional comfort into light plantar stimulation.

Design direction:
- softer than VITAL nodes
- distributed stimulation
- no painful pressure points
- Z-PsyFoot and Z-Reflex review required

### 6. FEMME Emotional Comfort Layer
Purpose:
Translate emotional state into comfort geometry.

Design direction:
- calming texture
- soft contact transitions
- breathable form
- sensory personalization

## Required Agent Route
Hermes -> Z-Research -> Z-FemmeBiomech -> Z-PsyFoot -> Z-Bio -> Z-Physics -> Z-CAD -> Z-Printability -> Z-Sim -> Z-Claims -> Z-Patent

# Shared P001 Rules

## No Printing Yet
P001 is documentation and design logic only.

## No Final CAD Yet
Any visual AI or Meshy output is concept only.

## No Medical Claims
Forbidden:
- treats injury
- cures pain
- prevents injury
- regulates hormones
- guarantees anxiety relief
- guarantees cortisol reduction
- medical diagnosis

Allowed:
- supports comfort
- supports recovery experience
- supports relaxation experience
- plantar pressure-informed design
- female biomechanics-informed comfort
- sensory stimulation map
- non-diagnostic indicators

## No Fixed Numbers Without Physics
Wall thickness, lattice density, stimulation height, and support geometry must be calculated by Z-Physics based on load case, user profile, and printability.

## Required Before Printing
Before any physical sample:
1. Research review
2. Prior art review
3. Patent notes
4. CAD concept
5. Printability checklist
6. Z-Sim validation plan
7. Claims safety review
8. Sultan approval
