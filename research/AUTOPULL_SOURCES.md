# ZILFIT Research Autopull Sources v0.1

Purpose:
Allow Z-Research to automatically pull recent research metadata from trusted public scientific indexes.

Sources:

1. PubMed / NCBI E-utilities
Use for:
- plantar pressure
- foot nerves
- foot massage
- autonomic nervous system
- HRV
- recovery
- female gait
- biomechanics
- reflexology clinical studies

2. OpenAlex
Use for:
- global university research
- engineering papers
- 3D printed footwear
- TPU lattice
- gait biomechanics
- smart insoles
- materials
- design research

3. Crossref
Use for:
- DOI metadata
- publisher records
- publication dates
- title and journal metadata
- prior art research discovery

Rule:
Autopulled results are not facts by default.
They are evidence candidates until reviewed.

No result may update:
- CAD
- claims
- patent language
- print instructions

until reviewed by:
Hermes -> Z-Claims -> Z-Patent -> Z-CAD -> Z-Sim
