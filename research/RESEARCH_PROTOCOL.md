# ZILFIT Research Protocol v0.1

Purpose:
Define how ZILFIT agents collect, classify, and apply research without hallucination.

Core rule:
Research does not become design, claim, patent language, or print instruction until classified and reviewed.

## Research Categories

1. Plantar Pressure
Research about pressure distribution, gait, foot loading, balance, and foot-ground interaction.

2. Footwear Biomechanics
Research about walking, running, recovery footwear, arch support, heel strike, toe-off, and comfort.

3. Female Biomechanics
Research about pelvis, Q-angle, female gait, foot shape, pressure distribution, and footwear differences.

4. Neuro-Sensory Stimulation
Research about mechanical stimulation, massage, foot stimulation, autonomic nervous system, HRV, relaxation, and recovery indicators.

5. Reflexology-Inspired Sensory Mapping
Research about reflexology and foot maps. Used only as sensory design inspiration unless clinically validated.

6. Materials and 3D Printing
Research about TPU, lattice, gyroid, auxetic structures, FDM, SLS, MJF, 3MF/STL, printability, durability, and flexible footwear.

7. Patent Prior Art
Patents, products, papers, or systems that may overlap with ZILFIT.

8. Claims and Regulation
Research about what can or cannot be said in wellness, medical, recovery, and footwear products.

---

# Evidence Levels

## HIGH
Systematic review, meta-analysis, standard, regulatory document, or repeated findings across strong studies.

## MEDIUM
Peer-reviewed study, controlled experiment, strong engineering paper, or validated product documentation.

## LOW
Single study, small sample, non-peer-reviewed technical note, market article, or early-stage evidence.

## HYPOTHESIS
Interesting but not verified. Can guide experiments but not product claims.

---

# Required Research Entry Format

Every research entry must include:

- date
- topic
- source_title
- source_url
- source_year
- evidence_level
- finding
- relevance_to_zilfit
- affected_agents
- design_rule_if_any
- claim_allowed
- claim_forbidden
- open_questions
- status

Status options:
- NEW
- REVIEWED
- APPLIED_TO_AGENT
- BLOCKED
- NEEDS_MORE_EVIDENCE

---

# Application Rules

## To update Z-CAD
Research must include a design-relevant rule:
- geometry
- pressure
- material
- thickness
- lattice
- comfort
- printability

## To update Z-Claims
Research must include:
- exact allowed wording
- forbidden wording
- evidence level

## To update Z-Patent
Research must identify:
- novelty risk
- prior art overlap
- possible differentiator

## To update Z-Sim
Research must include:
- measurable test
- pass/fail threshold
- risk condition

---

# Forbidden Behavior

Agents must not:
- invent sources
- cite inaccessible papers as if read
- turn hypothesis into fact
- claim treatment without validation
- use reflexology as medical proof
- use market examples as scientific proof
- approve design without Z-Sim
- approve claims without Z-Claims

---

# Daily Research Workflow

1. Z-Research collects sources.
2. Hermes classifies each source.
3. Z-Claims reviews wording.
4. Z-Patent checks novelty risk.
5. Z-CAD extracts design rules.
6. Z-Sim defines validation tests.
7. Sultan approves strategic changes.

No automatic application without approval.
